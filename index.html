An Open Letter to Anyone and No One

If you had told me when I was younger that I would some day be writing online I would have cringed at the thought. If you had told me earlier this year the answer would have been the same… yet here we are.

I suppose this has come about as a way for me to express and share my thoughts in a manor that I hope will find an audience of like minded people. As the title suggests this will be an honest account, perhaps even the first one I have put out publicly despite not being my first attempt at expression online.

Some things you should know about me before we go any further:

I am a deeply depressive person and not for a lack of trying. There is a notion that the four pillars; exercise, sleep, diet and relationships; will fix everything and well I would like to offer a counter to that in the form of my own mental health. I have struggled with issues such as identity, addiction, nihilism and have been clinically diagnosed with PTSD and depression.

I am a very extreme person I have come to find there is no safe middle ground for me, I am either all in or all out.

Notable things to date:

- I was intoxicated everyday for 2 years and then did another 2 completely sober
- I have my IRD number tattooed on the back of my neck
- I have been writing since 2018
- Worked my way from bartender to business insights analyst without a degree
- Started a small designer clothing label called SAD Collection
- Hosted a podcast called “Unhappy Hour”
- Have a vlog called “A Dull Life”

It is strange to distil your life into a few key bullet points I like to try and think of life as chapters in a book even down to the level of giving them titles. You will come to learn that I am not without my intricacies and that my internal dialogue is often far deeper than anything I dear articulate, words once spoken can not be withdrawn. This project however will reveal my inner most world, so welcome.

Truly,
SAD

I carry lanterns a bic and a dart lit,
